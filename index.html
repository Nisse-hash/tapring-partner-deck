How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

Tapring combines NFC technology with a simple web platform. No app download required for anyone on the receiving end.

01

The ring ships ready

Each Tapring contains an NFC chip with a unique URL embedded at the factory. A permanent, tamper-proof digital identity in a piece of jewelry.

02

Recipient claims their ring

They tap their phone to the ring, land on their personal page, and log in to take ownership. The whole process takes under two minutes.

03

They customize, you forget

The owner manages everything from a clean dashboard. You never touch it again. Their profile lives in the ring, updated in real time, forever.

Capabilities

One ring, two powerful modes.

Every Tapring owner chooses how their ring behaves. They can switch at any time — no hardware change, no reissue.
